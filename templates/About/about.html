What is DSGBase?

DSGBase is a personalized data management system designed to securely store and organize personal identity records such as full name, region, and ID number. It provides a private environment where users can manage their information with clarity and control.

How it Works

DSGBase allows users to input and store essential personal data in a structured format. The platform is organized into core sections like Description, Base, and Settings, which help navigate and manage data effectively. Each record is associated with identifiable attributes, such as region or ID, ensuring that the information is both meaningful and retrievable.

Who is it for?

DSGBase is built for general users who want a simple yet powerful tool for managing personal information. Whether you're an individual looking to organize identity-related data or a small group managing private records, DSGBase is designed to be intuitive and user-focused.

Security & Privacy

All data is stored locally on the device where DSGBase is used. It is not connected to the internet or any external servers, making it fully private and non-shareable by design. Your information stays with you — and only with you.

Technology Stack

DSGBase is developed using a blend of low-level and web technologies — C, Python, and JavaScript — to ensure efficiency, flexibility, and responsiveness.

Created By

DSGBase was developed by !!D7FEX and Gurjaka with the goal of providing a clean, offline-first personal database experience for everyone.
